Learn more about the hidden biases
and assumptions that we all carry during this
interactive training. Speakers Seisei Tatebe-Goddu and
Hannah Allison will discuss techniques in which we can
recognize and work with our biases.
We will be discussing methods such as the “Flower of Power” which explores the basis of cultural differences. We will use this activity to understand where we stand versus where others stand in order to evaluate the biases we may carry.
